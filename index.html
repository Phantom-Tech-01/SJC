About Us

Who We Are

SJC Innovators is a student-led club dedicated to coding, design, and technology. We focus on software development, game development, hackathons, and 3D web experiences. Our mission is to foster creativity, problem-solving, and innovation.

What We Do

  • Host hackathons, coding challenges, and workshops
  • Work on cutting-edge projects in AI, web, and app development
  • Collaborate with tech enthusiasts to build real-world applications
